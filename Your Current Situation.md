* **Mindset:** Feeling stressed, demotivated, and lacking clarity and self-worth.
* **Physical Health:** Overweight, feeling low energy, and struggling with bad habits.
* **Study:** No motivation for JEE prep, unclear goals, and inconsistent study routines.

### **Proposed Solution:**
1. **Daily Morning Routine:**
	* Wake up at a consistent time.
	* Include 5-10 minutes of light stretching or a short walk.
	* Spend a few minutes on mindfulness or journaling to set positive intentions.
2. **Physical Health Goals:**
	
	* Start with small, manageable activities, like a 15-minute walk daily.
	* Gradually incorporate light strength exercises as you build stamina.
	* Focus on balanced, healthy meals, and reduce junk food gradually.
3. **Study Plan:**
	
	* Break your JEE prep into daily, focused sessions.
	* Set specific goals for each study session (e.g., one topic at a time, practice problems).
	* Keep sessions short and focused to avoid burnout.
4. **Mindset & Clarity:**
	
	* Reflect daily on your progress and set small, achievable goals.
	* Use journaling to track your feelings and celebrate small wins.
	* Gradually build self-compassion and reduce negative self-talk.
